**Pizza Specification Document**

**1. Product Name**
Classic Oven-Baked Pizza

**2. Description**
A flat, round baked dish consisting of a yeast-leavened dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and customizable toppings. The pizza is baked at high temperature to achieve a crisp crust and evenly melted toppings.

**3. Size & Dimensions**

* Small: 8–10 inches (serves 1–2)
* Medium: 11–12 inches (serves 2–3)
* Large: 13–15 inches (serves 3–5)

**4. Crust Types**

* Thin Crust: Crisp and light
* Hand-Tossed: Balanced thickness with soft interior
* Thick Crust (Pan/Deep Dish): Dense and fluffy
* Stuffed Crust: Cheese-filled edges

**5. Dough Composition**

* Wheat flour (refined or whole wheat)
* Water
* Yeast
* Salt
* Olive oil (optional)
* Sugar (optional, for fermentation support)

**6. Sauce**

* Base: Tomato puree or crushed tomatoes
* Seasoning: Salt, garlic, oregano, basil, black pepper
* Optional variations: White sauce, pesto, barbecue sauce

**7. Cheese**

* Primary: Mozzarella (low or high moisture)
* Optional blends: Cheddar, Parmesan, Provolone

**8. Toppings (Customizable)**

* Vegetables: Onion, capsicum, mushroom, tomato, olives, corn
* Proteins: Chicken, pepperoni, sausage, paneer
* Herbs & Garnish: Oregano, chili flakes, basil leaves

**9. Baking Specifications**

* Oven temperature: 220–280°C
* Baking time: 8–15 minutes depending on crust thickness
* Bake until crust is golden brown and cheese is melted and bubbling

**10. Nutritional Information (Approx. per slice)**

* Calories: 200–350 kcal
* Carbohydrates: 25–40 g
* Protein: 8–15 g
* Fat: 8–18 g

**11. Storage & Shelf Life**

* Fresh consumption recommended
* Refrigeration: Up to 2–3 days
* Reheating: Oven at 180°C for 5–7 minutes

**12. Quality Standards**

* Evenly baked crust with no raw dough
* Fresh, high-quality ingredients
* Balanced distribution of toppings
* No excessive oil or sogginess

**13. Serving Suggestions**

* Serve hot
* Pair with soft drinks, juices, or dips (garlic sauce, chili sauce)
