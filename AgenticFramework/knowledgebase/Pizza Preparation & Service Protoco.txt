**Pizza Preparation & Service Protocol**

**1. Objective**
To define standardized procedures for preparing, assembling, baking, and serving pizza to ensure consistent quality, taste, and presentation.

**2. Scope**
Applies to all kitchen staff involved in pizza preparation, from ingredient handling to final service.

**3. Required Equipment**

* Dough mixer or mixing bowl
* Rolling pin or dough stretcher
* Pizza peel
* Baking oven (deck oven, conveyor oven, or wood-fired oven)
* Pizza cutter or rocker blade
* Ingredient storage containers (food-grade)

**4. Ingredient Handling Guidelines**

* Store perishable items (cheese, meats, sauces) at 1–5°C
* Keep vegetables washed, cut, and stored in sealed containers
* Use FIFO (First In, First Out) method for inventory rotation
* Ensure dough is proofed at room temperature before use

**5. Dough Preparation Process**

* Mix ingredients until smooth and elastic
* Allow fermentation for 1–24 hours depending on recipe
* Divide into equal portions and shape into balls
* Rest dough for elasticity before stretching

**6. Pizza Assembly Steps**

1. Dust surface with flour to prevent sticking
2. Stretch dough evenly to desired size
3. Apply sauce in a circular motion, leaving edges clean
4. Add cheese evenly across surface
5. Distribute toppings uniformly to avoid overloading

**7. Baking Procedure**

* Preheat oven to required temperature (typically 230–300°C)
* Transfer pizza using peel onto baking surface
* Bake until crust is crisp and cheese is fully melted
* Rotate pizza if needed for even cooking

**8. Quality Control Checklist**

* Crust should be evenly browned with no burnt spots
* Cheese should be melted and slightly golden
* Toppings must be properly cooked
* Pizza should not be soggy or overly dry

**9. Cutting & Serving**

* Allow pizza to rest for 1–2 minutes after baking
* Cut into equal slices (4, 6, or 8 depending on size)
* Serve on clean plates or in takeaway boxes
* Provide condiments such as oregano and chili flakes

**10. Hygiene & Safety Standards**

* Wash hands before handling food
* Use gloves when assembling pizzas
* Sanitize surfaces regularly
* Avoid cross-contamination between raw and cooked ingredients

**11. Customer Service Guidelines**

* Serve pizza hot and fresh
* Confirm order accuracy before delivery
* Handle special dietary requests (e.g., vegetarian, no cheese) carefully

**12. Waste Management**

* Dispose of expired ingredients immediately
* Minimize food waste through proper portioning
* Recycle packaging materials where possible

**13. Continuous Improvement**

* Collect customer feedback regularly
* Adjust recipes and processes based on demand trends
* Train staff periodically for skill enhancement
